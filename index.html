FAQ

  • Is PowerPlay free to play?
    Yes! PowerPlay: Ice Hockey PvP Game is completely free to download and play. There are optional in-game purchases for customization items, but they are not required to enjoy the game.
  • Can I play PowerPlay offline?
    Yes! You can play in single-player mode against AI opponents without an internet connection. However, PvP mode and tournaments require an internet connection to connect with real players.
  • What devices is the game available on?
    PowerPlay is available for Android devices. We are working on an iOS version, so stay tuned for updates!
  • How does the multiplayer mode work?
    In multiplayer (PvP) mode, you will be matched with opponents based on your skill level and ranking. You can play one-on-one matches or join tournaments to test your skills against the best players worldwide.
  • Can I upgrade my player and team?
    Absolutely! You can train and upgrade your player’s skating, shooting, defense, and stamina to improve performance. Additionally, you can unlock new jerseys, gear, and equipment to customize your team.
  • What game modes are available?
    PowerPlay features multiple game modes:
    • Quick Match – Jump into a game instantly and test your skills.
    • PvP Mode – Compete against real players worldwide.
    • Tournament Mode – Battle through rounds to become the ultimate hockey champion.
    • Training Mode – Practice and refine your hockey techniques.
  • Will there be more updates and new features?
    Yes! We are actively working on new game modes, more customization options, and additional hockey arenas to keep the experience fresh. Stay tuned for future updates!
  • How can I report a bug or give feedback?
    We value your feedback! If you encounter a bug or have suggestions for improvements, you can contact our support team through the settings menu in the game or visit our official website powerluckstar.com.
